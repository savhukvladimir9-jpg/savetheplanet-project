Our Mission / Наша миссия

SaveThePlanet (STP) mission is to fund and support measurable environmental projects worldwide — reforestation, ocean cleanup, and sustainable tech. We combine blockchain transparency and community governance to ensure funds reach real projects and produce verifiable impact.

How we act

  • 2% of every transaction → Eco Fund
  • Community voting on projects (DAO)
  • Transparency reports and blockchain-backed receipts
