SaveThePlanet — Marketplace

1. What is Marketplace?

SaveThePlanet Marketplace is the ecosystem store where users can use STP tokens to purchase:
  • Carbon credits
  • Eco-products
  • Merchandise & collectibles
  • Service access & upgrades
  • DAO community items
Every purchase directly supports environmental restoration actions.

2. Main Marketplace Categories

Carbon Offsetting
Users can buy verified CO₂ offset credits to reduce personal or business emissions.
Eco-Merch
Clothing, accessories, and digital merch supporting eco-missions.
NFT Certificates
Digital proof of support for real-world environmental projects.
Project Marketplace
Funding marketplace where holders choose which global eco-initiatives receive financing.
DAO Services
Premium membership, voting boosts, tools and ecosystem utilities.

3. Token Use Cases in Marketplace

  • Payments for goods & services
  • Staking for improved marketplace perks
  • Discounts for STP token holders
  • Platform fee buyback & burn mechanics
Token usage in the marketplace increases real demand and supports long-term growth.

4. Revenue & Sustainability

Marketplace revenue is distributed as:
  • 40% — Direct project support
  • 20% — Token buyback & burn
  • 20% — Ecosystem development
  • 10% — Operations
  • 10% — Reserves
This structure maintains high sustainability and real economic value.
