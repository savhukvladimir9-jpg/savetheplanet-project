SaveThePlanet — Tokenomics

Token Core Model

SaveThePlanet token is designed to support ecological projects around the world. Its tokenomics ensures sustainability, community development, and transparent resource allocation through blockchain.

Total Supply

Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 Tokens (Fixed, no additional minting)

Distribution

  • 40% — Community and Rewards
  • 25% — Eco Projects Fund
  • 15% — Team & Development
  • 10% — Marketing & Partnerships
  • 10% — Liquidity and Exchange Listings

Tax System (Buy / Sell)

  • 2% — Goes into Eco Support Fund
  • 1% — Distributed to token holders (Reflection)
  • 1% — Marketing & development
Tax applies only to trading operations — not transfers.

Token Utility

  • Voting on ecological funding proposals
  • Rewards for active participation
  • Access to exclusive events and governance tools
  • Staking and long-term holding incentives

Smart Contract Principles

  • Fully ERC-20 compatible
  • Immutable supply
  • Transparent on-chain tax distribution
  • Upgradeable through community governance
