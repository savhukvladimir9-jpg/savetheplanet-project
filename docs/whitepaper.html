SaveThePlanet — Whitepaper

1. Introduction

SaveThePlanet is a decentralized blockchain ecosystem aimed at financing, supporting, and accelerating real-world environmental projects using transparent token mechanics and global community participation.

2. Vision & Mission

Mission: Restore ecological balance through collective funding, blockchain accountability, and real measurable impact.

Vision: Create the world’s largest decentralized platform where every token holder becomes a contributor to saving the planet.

3. What Makes Us Unique

  • Real environmental impact — measurable and verifiable.
  • Blockchain ensures transparency and trust.
  • Community earns rewards for ecological actions.
  • Token backed by real-world sustainability projects.
  • Funding is fully traceable.

4. Token Model & Economics

Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 STP
Token Standard: ERC-20 (or compatible)

Distribution:
  • 40% — Environmental Project Funding
  • 20% — Staking & Rewards
  • 15% — Development & Security
  • 10% — Global Marketing
  • 10% — Treasury & Ecosystem Growth
  • 5% — Strategic Partnerships

5. Token Utilities

  • Voting on ecological projects
  • Access to staking rewards
  • Governance participation
  • Project funding support
  • Marketplace exchange value

6. Roadmap

Phase 1 — Foundation
  • Concept development
  • Token launch
  • Initial partners
Phase 2 — Expansion
  • Project grant selection
  • Mobile application development
  • Marketing and community growth
Phase 3 — Global Scale
  • International eco-network
  • Real-world measurable sustainability reports

7. Core Team

Blockchain developers, environmental analysts, financial architects, and marketing specialists with real-world experience in large projects.

8. Risks & Challenges

  • Market volatility
  • Regulatory uncertainty
  • Global eco-operation logistics
  • Scalability challenges
The project mitigates these with governance, audits, community reporting, and transparent budgeting.

9. Conclusion

SaveThePlanet represents a new standard for transparent, globally-scalable eco-initiatives powered by blockchain and community.
